Shazam

20
Mai, 2014

Created by: Shazam Entertainment Limited

Version: 7.6.0

Size: 28.1 MB

Rating: 4+

Compatibility: 6.0 or later
I paid $5 for this app a few years ago..so why is it that I'm seeing advertisements disguised as "trending now" pop up while I wait for it to listen to a song? Maybe because this app has turned into just another promotion-heavy interface that connects you to either a site full of ads (FB, Ytube, etc) or a paying site (ie. iTunes). With each update they seem to slip in more and more ways to advertise just short of displaying a full time pop-up bar across the screen. What about the actual function of the app to match music? Well, unless you're listening to what's being burned into your ears by radio stations that don't seem to have much more than an hours worth of the same repetitive songs, you can expect to wait several seconds before it finds a match. And that's only if the song is an American song. In English. God forbid you ask it to match a Spanish song-even if it's been played out in the radio more than a Pit Bull remix. With satelite radio being almost the standard, I would expect this app to be able to tap into the vastness that is the internet to match a tune. Especially when I can manually look up the chorus in iTunes and find it's true title. I mean, the app's main ally is iTunes! Is it too much to ask that it comb through iTunes' database to match a song before telling me a match couldn't be found? I guess that's too much coding for a measely $5 app. ..or maybe they're too busy figuring out new ways to promote crap.
