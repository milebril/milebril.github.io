Free Video Dowloader

17
Mai, 2014

Created by: zhou Xingfa

Version: 1.2.1

Size: 23.4 MB

Rating: 4+

Compatibility: 5.0 or later
To say this app is fantastic would be an understatement. All I wanted to do was save my Facebook videos to my iPad, but as we all know, the "Videos" album is non-existent on Facebook Mobile which makes having a video downloader virtually useless if you're only using it for downloading Fb vids. To find/watch a video you posted six months ago you actually have to scroll (and scroll, and scroll..) down your timeline to the exact day you uploaded it, which is extremely frustrating for anyone. Especially if you post on Fb a lot and/or your wifi connection is low. And you can't watch videos at all on Safari because Flash doesn't support Apple products! I've literally been searching for an app like this for more than two years. Not only does it let you watch your Facebook videos easily, it let's you save them to your camera roll as well! And if that's not exponentially awesome enough, the downloading process is ULTRA fast. Fastest downloader I've ever used, and trust me I've used them ALL. lol. This one crashes once every five minutes or so, but I'm not complaining. It does EXACTLY as the description says it does, and more. Aside from the occasional crash, I haven't had a single problem out of it. But if you do, I would suggest either reinstalling, or just waiting for the next update cuz you won't find a video downloader better than this one!
